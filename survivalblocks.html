Essential Survival Blocks

Wood Planks
The foundation of survival! Craft from any wood logs. Essential for tools, shelter, and countless recipes. Always gather wood first!
Cobblestone
Durable building material obtained by mining stone. Perfect for tools, furnaces, and strong structures. Mine with wooden pickaxe or better.
Dirt
Abundant and versatile! Use for farming, temporary structures, and landscaping. Can be converted to farmland for crops.
Grass Block
Spreads naturally and supports plant growth. Animals spawn on grass. Essential for creating sustainable food sources.
Furnace
Craft with 8 cobblestone. Smelt ores, cook food, and create essential materials. Your gateway to advanced crafting!
Chest
Storage solution crafted from 8 wood planks. Combine two for a large chest. Organize your items efficiently!
Crafting Table
Made from 4 wood planks. Unlocks 3x3 crafting grid for advanced recipes. Your first priority after gathering wood!
Glass
Smelt sand in furnace. Perfect for windows, greenhouses, and decorative builds. Lets light through while keeping mobs out.
Coal Block
Mine stone with black flecks using any pickaxe. The fundamental fuel for furnaces and crafting essential Torches. Crucial for smelting, cooking, and lighting up your base against the dark.
